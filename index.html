About me

Originally from northern Russia, I started my journey to UX / HTI field in August 2016 by becoming a master degree student at Tampere University (formerly Tampere University of Technology). Being passionate about user-centered and graphic design and design research, I always strive for details and simple, but creative solutions.

So far I managed to participate in a couple of fairly big projects such as Living Lab Bus, where I was mostly focusing on developer experience, and worked with Intopalo on data governance tool. I also dabble at digital art and spend my spare time creating environmental digital paintings and sketches or practicing visual design.

I recently have been graduated from Tampere University and currently I am searching for job openings related to UX/UI design and research. I want to join the team of fellow designers and developers where I can enhance my skills, solve interesting and complex problems, meet new fascinating people and learn new technologies and methodologies.
