About me

Originally from northern Russia, I started my journey at UX / HTI field in August 2016 by becoming a master's degrees student at Tampere University of Technology. Being passionate about user-centered and graphic design and design research, I always strive for details and creative solutions.

So far I managed to participate in a couple of fairly big projects such as Living Lab Bus, where I was mostly focusing on developer experience, and worked with Intopalo on data governance tool. I am currently finishing up my studies and searching for an open UX- and/or UI-related position. I desire to join the team of fellow designers and developers where I could realize my potential, meet new interesting people and learn new technologies and methodologies.

I also dabble at digital art and spend my spare time creating environmental digital paintings and sketches or practicing visual design.
